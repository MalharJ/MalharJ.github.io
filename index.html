Hi there, I'm Malhar Jere!

I'm a graduate student at UC San Diego, where I work in the incredible ACESLab advised by the awesome Professor Farinaz Koushanfar . Previously, I graduated from UIUC with a BS in Electrical Engineering, where I did research in Paul Kwiat's group working with satellites and quantum optics. My interests primarily lie in understanding neural networks and devising tools to interpret their behavior and spot unknown biases. I'm also interested in machine learning security and robustness, and making machines understand the world much the same way humans do. Outside of work my hobbies I love hiking, cooking and reading.
